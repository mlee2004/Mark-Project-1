Home

Our ethos is to cultivate progress through dedicated research. We combine modern science with traditional breeding methods to create top quality and high yielding varieties. Space Eye Agro also works with regard to technical developments suggested by research institutions, applying the latest science to generate better solutions for customers and growers. With global drive to have food produced and supplied in a state that is safe for human consumption, we have resolved to certify our production systems to GlobalGAP and SEDEX.

About Us

With offices in Nairobi – Kenya, SpaceEye Agro is a leader in the growing of fresh agricultural produce. We bring expertise, commitment and with support of state – of – the – art technology we breed, develop, produce and market varieties of vegetable crops around the world.
