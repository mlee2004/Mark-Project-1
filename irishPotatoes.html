Irish Potatoes

The Irish potato, Solanumtuberosum, is the second most important staple crop grown in Kenya, after maize, and before rice.
Irish Potatoes are extremely good. Irish potato tubers contain zero fat, sodium & cholesterol and packed with protein, slow release carbohydrates and vitamins and minerals, not to mention health promoting phytonutrients.
Potato is a major food crop that is grown in over 100 countries around the world . Global demand (and more so, local demand) is rising due to rapid urbanization creating a growing middle class population and the increasing appeal of fast foods e.g. crisps (chips) and French fries.
